# 30-Day Content Plan — Kids Nursery Rhymes Channel ($10k/Month Roadmap)

**Reference channel:** [NuNu Tv – Nursery Rhymes](https://www.youtube.com/@NuNuTVNurseryRhymes/shorts) — 28.4M subscribers, 14.6B+ views. 3D-animated toddler content: classic rhymes remade (Wheels on the Bus, Bingo, Five Little Monkeys), original "good habits" songs (Boo Boo Song, Playground Song), bright colors, cute baby characters, heavy focus on Social-Emotional Learning (SEL).

---

## 1. The honest math first (read this before anything else)

Kids content is marked **"Made for Kids" (MFK)** on YouTube. That changes the economics:

| Revenue stream | Typical rate (MFK) | What $10k/mo requires |
|---|---|---|
| Shorts ads | ~$0.01–$0.07 RPM | 150M–1B shorts views/mo (not realistic alone) |
| Long-form ads (compilations) | ~$0.30–$1.50 RPM | 7M–30M long-form views/mo |
| YouTube Kids app views | Included above | — |
| Music streaming (Spotify/Apple kids playlists) | ~$3–4 per 1k streams | Bonus stream, compounds over time |
| Licensing/distribution (Roku kids apps, airlines, IPTV) | Deal-based | Possible once you have a 50+ video library |

**The model that actually hits $10k/mo** (this is exactly what NuNu TV, Cocomelon, and ChuChu TV do):

> **Shorts = discovery engine. Long-form compilations = the money.**

Every short is a 40–60s song clip. Every 6–8 shorts get stitched into a 15–30 minute long-form compilation ("Boo Boo Song + More Nursery Rhymes"). Toddlers watch compilations on repeat on TVs — long watch time, mid-roll ads, real RPM. Shorts feed subscribers and algorithm signals into those compilations.

**Realistic timeline:** Months 1–3 = build library + find your breakout song. Months 4–8 = double down on what hits, compilations compound. $10k/mo typically lands months 6–12 *if* you get 1–2 breakout songs. This 30-day plan is the machine that makes that possible — not a guarantee of $10k in 30 days.

## 2. Channel identity (lock this in Days 1–2, never change it)

- **Cast:** 2 recurring toddler characters + 1 animal sidekick (e.g., "Toto & Lily + Momo the cat"). Recurring characters = brand = merch/licensing later. NuNu TV's entire moat is character recognition.
- **Visual style:** Bright saturated 3D (or high-quality AI-assisted 2.5D), rounded shapes, big eyes, soft outlines. Consistent color palette in every thumbnail.
- **Audio signature:** One 3-second sonic logo/jingle at the start of every video. Toddlers recognize sound before visuals.
- **Content pillars (rotate all month):**
  1. **Classic rhymes remade** (proven demand: Wheels on the Bus, Baby Shark-adjacent, Old MacDonald)
  2. **Good-habit songs** (brush teeth, potty, boo boo, sharing, bath time — parents *search* for these)
  3. **Learning songs** (colors, numbers 1–10, ABC, shapes, animal sounds)
  4. **SEL/feelings songs** (don't be scared, saying sorry, first day fears — NuNu's differentiator)

---

## 3. Production pipeline (repeatable, ~2–4 hrs per short)

1. **Lyrics/script** — 8–12 lines, one repeated hook, simple words, call-and-response structure. (AI-draft, human-polish.)
2. **Music** — Suno/Udio or a Fiverr kids-music producer ($30–80/song). Keep tempo 90–110 BPM, key of C.
3. **Visuals** — Pick ONE workflow and master it:
   - Budget: AI image gen (consistent character sheets) + animation tools (Runway/Kling/Viewmax-style image-to-video) + CapCut
   - Better: Blender/Cartoon Animator with purchased toddler rigs ($100–300 one-time)
4. **Edit** — 9:16, hard cut every 2–3s, on-screen lyrics in big rounded font, loop-friendly ending (last frame ≈ first frame → rewatches).
5. **Metadata** — Title format: `Song Name 🎵 Nursery Rhymes & Kids Songs | [Channel Name]`. Mark **Made for Kids**. 3 hashtags: #nurseryrhymes #kidssongs #babysongs.

**Cadence:** 1 short/day minimum (2/day from Week 3 if pipeline allows) + 1 long-form compilation per week from Week 2.

## 7. Path from Day 30 → $10k/month

1. **Months 2–3:** 2 shorts/day, 2 compilations/week. Target 100-video library. Every hit gets 3+ remixes (NuNu has ~10 versions of Wheels on the Bus — that's not laziness, that's the strategy).
2. **Month 3+:** Translate top 10 songs into Spanish + Hindi + Portuguese audio tracks (YouTube multi-language audio) — kids content is language-light; this 3–5x's the addressable audience free.
3. **Months 4–6:** Long-form compilations become 30–60 min "TV mode" videos. This is where 80% of revenue comes from.
4. **Months 6+:** Licensing outreach (kids streaming apps, Roku channels), merch (character plush via print-on-demand), music streaming royalties stacking.
5. **The $10k composition at maturity:** ~$6–8k long-form AdSense + $1–2k Shorts/Reels/TikTok + $1–2k streaming/licensing/merch.

**The one rule:** consistency beats brilliance in this niche. Toddlers rewatch; parents press play on what's familiar. Ship every day for 30 days, measure, remix winners.
